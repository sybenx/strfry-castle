The Outer Lands are open to all. Events here survive until the next raid, at which point strangers' notes older than the standing limit are swept away — the invited, the followed, and the favored are never touched.

A note on private messages: gift-wrapped DMs (NIP-59) are signed by random one-time keys, so this relay cannot tell the Lord's own messages from a stranger's. All gift wraps age out at the next raid like any other Outer Lands event. For permanent, private message storage, use a dedicated relay built for that purpose.

No Nostr signer extension was found. Signing in requires a NIP-07 browser extension so you can prove your identity without ever handing the castle your keys. Common ones:

  • Alby
  • nos2x
  • Nostr Connect (mobile signers like Amber)
